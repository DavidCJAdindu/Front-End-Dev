About Lavish Photography

David CJ Lavish owns and runs Lavish Photography. With over 40 years of experience and numerous awards in both family photography and portraiture, including Kodak and Fuji Photographer of the Year, he runs the studio with his brother, Ole.

David CJ Lavish, Owner & Photographer

David is a member of the Irish Professional Photographer's Association and is regarded as one of Ireland's best professional photographers. With his years of experience he has photographed everything from newborns to celebrities and everything in between.

His philosophy is to capture moments you can treasure forever and he strives to deliver the best experience to tell your story.

Ole Gunnar, Photographer

Ole brings the studio into its third generation.

Specialising in youth portraits, he views studio photography as an experience and endeavours to create a fun and relaxed atmosphere that will bring out the best in every child. He not only creates beautiful photographs but great memories you can share with your family for years to come.
